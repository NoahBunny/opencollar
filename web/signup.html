Welcome, Lion.

This wizard sets up a new mesh on this relay. Your Bunny will join with an invite code at the end.

You'll configure (each step is optional except your key):

  • Your Lion public key (required)
  • Payment email scanning (Interac, Wise, etc.)
  • Default rules: tribute, bedtime, screen time
  • Default subscription tier preset

Everything here can be changed later from Lion's Share. Nothing is final until the last step.

[K]
Your Lion key

This is the RSA public key from Lion's Share → Advanced → Export Key. Your private key never leaves your phone.

This key signs every order you issue. The relay verifies signatures but never sees your private key.

Used as a recovery code on the Collar's lock screen if Lion's Share is unreachable. If left blank, the relay will mint a random one.
